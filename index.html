Common questions

The honest answers, before you apply.

Why only 10 founding members?

Because boutique means boutique. We'd rather serve ten operators exceptionally well than fifty adequately. After the cohort closes, pricing moves to standard and the door reopens at a different shape.

What does $199/month actually get me?

A full podcast and rooms representation service: positioning, sourcing, hand-crafted outreach, scheduling, prep docs, and follow-up. You show up, you be brilliant. Everything around the appearance is ours to handle.

What happens after the founding cohort fills?

Existing members keep their $199 rate forever. New members, when we re-open, come in at the then-current rate, which will be higher. The lock-in is the point.

What if I'm rejected?

You hear back either way, usually within 72 hours. If we're not the right fit right now, we'll tell you why. Most rejections are timing, not quality.

Is this a lead-gen agency?

No. We're not selling you leads, SEO, or growth-hack funnels. We're placing you in rooms where your expertise compounds. Different service, different outcomes.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Month to month, no contract. The rate lock is a promise on our end, not a leash on yours. Most members stay because it works, not because they have to.
